UnSCruz, the biggest and baddest Santa Cruz Regional event is BACK and is a two day event at the sprawling and beautiful Santa Cruz County Fairgrounds!

Join us as we bring the spirit of Burning Man to Santa Cruz County through radical self expression and radical inclusion. Get ready for two fun-filled DAYS of art, performance, music, circus arts, DJs, mutant vehicles, and more!

Bring your energy, whimsy, and enthusiasm to the Fairgrounds - a great private venue with tons of space for theme camps, two indoor music rooms, an outdoor grass amphitheater, and more!

This is a PACK IT IN, PACK IT OUT Event! Bring enough of your own food and water (no outside alcohol!) to survive for two days in a not-so-harsh coastal environment.

Will there be food sold at the event?
No, everyone needs to bring their own food and water to the event.
Will there be alcohol sold at the event?
There will be a limited amount of alcohol available.
I would like to volunteer, dj, bring an art installation, or a theme camp. Who do I contact?
Sign up by closing this FAQ window and looking for links to the forms at the bottom of the page.
Are pets allowed?
No
Where do I purchase tickets?
Online at unscruz2014.brownpapertickets.com or at the gate the day of the event.
Do children have a different ticket price than adults?
Children under 12 are free.
We don’t want to camp at UnSCruz, but would like to go for the day can we buy a day pass?
There is only one ticket price that includes parking and camping.
What should I bring?
Food, water, costumes, camping gear, and silly costumes.
Are barbeques allowed?
Yes, as long as they are off the ground.
Can I camp next to my car?
No. Camping will be inside the fairgrounds, cars will be in the parking lot.
What is the policy with Recreational Vehicles (RV’s)?
There will be a limited number of RV spots w/hookups for an additional $40, available first come.
Are there toilets?
Yes.
What kind of facilities do you provide for those in a wheelchair?
The event is wheelchair accessible, although much of the event takes place on the lawn area.
Can we have camp fires?
No
What about trash? Are there dumpsters for me to throw my trash in?
Yes, but like all Burning Man events, we strongly encourage you to be self-reliant, pack it in, and pack it out.
What time does the event end?
The event ends at 5 pm on Sunday.
