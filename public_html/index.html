UnSCruz, the biggest and baddest Santa Cruz Burner event is BACK and is the FIRST OFFICIAL BURNING MAN DECOMPRESSION Party in California!!

Join us as we bring the spirit of Burning Man to Santa Cruz through radical self expression and radical inclusion. Get ready for eight fun-filled hours of art, performance, music, circus arts, DJs, mutant vehicles, fire, and more!

Bring your energy, whimsy, and enthusiasm to Portuguese Hall - a great private venue with tons of space for theme camps, two indoor music rooms, a full bar, snack service, and more!
